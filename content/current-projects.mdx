## Where I'm Working Right Now

Product design and agentic AI have the same core problem: figuring out what a system should do and making sure it doesn't get in the user's way. That overlap is where I spend most of my time.

## The Stack

This is the toolkit. Every piece chosen because it collapses the gap between design intent and shipped product:

*   **Cursor & Claude Code:** AI-native development — I prompt, iterate, and ship production code without context-switching out of the design problem.
*   **Figma + MCP Pipelines:** Design tokens and components flow directly into running code. Zero handoff, zero drift.
*   **OpenClaw:** My autonomous agent (Lobo 🐺) runs billing, ops, and marketing workflows end-to-end. I built it. I use it daily. It works.
*   **Veo & Nano Banana:** Generative video and image models for brand assets at a speed and cost that would've required a production team.
